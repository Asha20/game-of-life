How to Play

Live cells are represented by white rectangles.

Neighbors of a cell are the 8 cells that are directly or diagonally adjacent to it.

A live cell will stay alive only if it has either 2 or 3 alive neighbors.

A dead cell will become alive if it has exactly 3 alive neighbors.

Left click to place a live cell.

Right click to remove a live cell.

After placing, press Advance to advance cells automatically.

Alternatively, press Advance 1 to advance a single generation.
